CS major, newly-found bread enthusiast. Familiar with Java, C, HTML, Javascript.


For our project, we decided to make a catalog website for bread. There are a crazy amount of different kinds of bread, and including all of them on one page would be too much, so data was pulled from statista.com to find the top breads that were eaten in the US. The design idea for the index and catalog was inspired by other modern commercial websites, keeping things big and simple. Bootstrap elements, especially panels, make up the vast majority of the index and catalog pages, making scaling much more dynamic.
